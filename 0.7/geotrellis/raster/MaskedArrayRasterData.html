MaskedArrayRasterData

final case class MaskedArrayRasterData(data: ArrayRasterData, col1: Int, row1: Int, col2: Int, row2: Int) extends LazyRasterData with Wrapper with Product with Serializable

NOTE: col1 and row1 (upper left) is inclusive, while col2 and row2 (lower right) is not. This means that the actual size of the "real data" here is just col2 - col1, and row2 - row1.
